# Training Grounds - Quick Reference Guide

## Overview
The Training Grounds section has been enhanced with powerful search, filtering, and schedule management capabilities to help you plan your training regimen efficiently.

---

## Main Features

### 1. 📋 Recent Training Sessions Display
The "Recent Training Sessions" section now displays your past workouts in a card-based format that's easy to scan and review.

**What you see:**
- Exercise name
- Training date (formatted with weekday)
- Sets, Reps, Weight metrics
- Training notes (if any)
- Edit button for future modifications

**Located:** Bottom of the Training Grounds page (desktop only)

---

### 2. 🔍 Technique Explorer (Sidebar Search)
Quickly find and preview techniques for today's workout without committing to your schedule.

**How to use:**
1. Select a specific training day (Monday-Sunday) in the sidebar
2. Type in the "Search scrolls..." field
3. Results appear instantly, showing:
   - Technique name
   - Difficulty level
   - Exercise type
4. Click "+" to add to today (appears as preview)

**Search matches:**
- Technique names
- Exercise types
- Target muscle groups

**Note:** You must select a specific day first. The "🔄 All" button doesn't allow adding (preview only).

---

### 3. ⏱️ Preview System (Non-Persistent)
Add techniques to see how they look in your training day without permanently committing.

**How it works:**
1. Click "+" in the Technique Explorer sidebar
2. Technique appears with a gold "Preview" badge
3. See your complete day layout before committing
4. Preview shows message: "⏱️ Preview only - commit in Training Schedule to persist"

**Important:** Preview techniques disappear when you:
- Refresh the page
- Navigate away
- Close and reopen the application

**To keep it permanently:** Add the technique through the Training Schedule Management drawer

---

### 4. ⚔️ Training Schedule Management (Drawer)
The official interface for permanently assigning techniques to your training days.

**How to open:**
Click "⚔️ Training Schedule" button in the sidebar

**Main features:**
- View and manage techniques assigned to each day
- Add techniques to specific days
- Remove techniques from days
- Apply advanced filters

---

## Advanced Filtering

### Three Filter Dimensions

All filters work together with **AND logic** - an exercise must match ALL selected filters to appear.

#### 1. 🏯 Realm Filter (Difficulty Level)
- **All**: Show all difficulty levels
- **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, **Advanced**: Shows only that level's techniques
- Helps focus on exercises matching your current progression

#### 2. 🎯 Path Filter (Training Methodology)
- **All**: Show all training approaches
- **Strength**, **Flexibility**, **Endurance**, etc.: Focus on specific training styles
- Helps organize by training focus

#### 3. 💪 Target Group Filter (Muscle Groups)
- **All**: Show all target groups
- **Upper Body**, **Lower Body**, **Core**, etc.: Focus on specific body regions
- Helps prevent overuse injuries by balancing muscle groups

#### Clearing Filters
Click "🔄 Clear All Filters" button to reset all selections at once.

## Understanding the Day Buttons

### In Sidebar (Training Schedule Navigator)
Shows all your training days with technique counts:
- **🔄 All**: Total count of all assigned techniques
- **Mon-Sun**: Count of techniques assigned to that day
- Numbers appear as small badges

### In Training Schedule Management Drawer

#### "All" Button
- **Purpose**: Browse complete technique library
- **Display**: Shows ALL available techniques (filtered by current selections)
- **Assignment**: Disabled - cannot add while viewing "All"  
- **Use case**: Exploring options or researching techniques

#### Day Buttons (Monday-Sunday)
- **Purpose**: Manage that specific day's techniques
- **Display**: Shows ONLY techniques assigned to that day
- **Empty state**: "No techniques scheduled for [Day] yet"
- **Assignment**: Enabled - can add or remove techniques
- **Guidance**: Sidebar prompts you to browse available techniques below

## User Workflows

### Workflow: Quick Single-Session Planning
Perfect for adding one-off techniques without committing:

1. Navigate to Training Grounds
2. In sidebar, click specific day (e.g., "Mon")
3. Type technique name in "Search scrolls..."
4. Click "+" on result
5. Technique appears with gold "Preview" badge
6. Do some exercises and close the page
7. Preview is gone (wasn't saved permanently)

**Result:** Ad-hoc session addition without cluttering your schedule

---

### Workflow: Building Your Weekly Schedule
Perfect for intentional, long-term schedule construction:

1. Click "⚔️ Training Schedule" in sidebar
2. Click specific day (e.g., "Monday")
3. (Optional) Apply filters to narrow options:
   - Select "Realm" → "Advanced"
   - Select "Path" → "Strength"
   - Select "Target Group" → "Legs"
4. Browse filtered techniques
5. Click "+" next to desired technique
6. Repeat for other days
7. Click "✕" to close drawer
8. Refresh page - assignments persist!

**Result:** Structured weekly schedule that's saved permanently

---

### Workflow: Hybrid Approach
Using both preview and permanent assignment:

1. Open sidebar search, add preview technique
2. View layout to confirm it fits your day
3. Open Training Schedule Management drawer
4. Select same day
5. Technique appears in "Add Techniques" list
6. Click "+" to promote from preview to permanent
7. Or just keep both visible (they look different)

**Result:** Flexible planning with final commitment step

## Visual Indicators

### Persisted Techniques (Permanently Assigned)
- 🎨 **Jade theme**: Green/teal border and background
-📍 **Location**: Both in daily display and drawer as "assigned"
- ✨ **Indicator**: Regular technique card, no special badge
- 💾 **Persistence**: Saved permanently, survives page refresh

### Preview Techniques (Temporary)
- 🎨 **Gold theme**: Gold border and background  
- 🏷️ **Badge**: Small gold "Preview" label in top-right corner
- ℹ️ **Message**: "⏱️ Preview only - commit in Training Schedule to persist"
- ❌ **Button**: "✕" for removal (separate from assigned techniques)
- ⏰ **Persistence**: Temporary, disappears on page refresh

## Tips & Tricks

### Tip 1: Use Filter Combinations
Combine all three filters for powerful results:
- Realm: "Advanced" + Path: "Strength" + Target: "Legs" = Advanced leg strength techniques

### Tip 2: Search While Filtering
Apply filters first, then search within results:
- Set Realm to "Beginner"
- Search "punch" for beginner-friendly punch techniques

### Tip 3: Clear Everything at Once
When you want a fresh start: Click "🔄 Clear All Filters" to reset all selections

### Tip 4: Preview Before Committing
Always preview techniques (gold badge) before formally assigning (drawer method)
- Make sure they fit your day
- Check for conflicts
- Verify target group balance

### Tip 5: Count Badges Show Totals
Day buttons show combined counts (persisted + preview) on same day
- Helps visualize your workload
- See which days are busier

### Tip 6: Use "All" for Research
Click "All" button in drawer to see full library
- Great for discovering new techniques
- Apply filters to browse by category
- Just don't refresh - your session won't persist!

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I use preview for actual training?**  
A: Yes! Preview techniques work perfectly during your training session. Just remember they won't be there after you refresh.

**Q: How do I save a preview technique?**  
A: Open Training Schedule Management drawer, select the same day, and click "+" on the technique. It will permanently assign and no longer show the gold Preview badge.

**Q: What happens if I have both preview and assigned versions?**  
A: Nothing bad! You'll see both - preview shows gold "Preview" badge, assigned shows normally. If you're duplicating, you can remove either one.

**Q: Can I see what's on "All" days?**  
A: Yes! Sidebar shows individual day counts. Click any day button to view that day's techniques.

**Q: Do filters apply to sidebar search too?**  
A: No. Sidebar search in Training Grounds is independent. The filters only apply inside the Training Schedule Management drawer.

**Q: What if I delete a technique by mistake?**  
A: Just click "+" again in the drawer to add it back. There's no permanent delete - only removal from your schedule.
